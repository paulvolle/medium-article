How to prepare good cookies in 10 minutes

This is a cheap version of cookies

Lists of ingredients

  • 100g of pain flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 400g of butter
  • 2 chocolate bars

Step 1: Prepare cookie dough

  1. Mix plain flour, eggs and butter into a plate
  2. Knead everything so that it becomes solid

Step 2: Make the cookies round

  • Take your cookie dough (don't eat it!)
  • Try to make some circles so that you obtain a little ball of cookie dough
